DomainSniper Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

DomainSniper does not collect personally identifiable information except for the following, strictly for functionality:

  • Email address for account and subscription purposes.
  • Optional domain search data to display availability results.

2. How We Use Data

Collected data is used only to:

  • Enable domain availability checks and display results.
  • Provide subscription services (Free, Silver, Gold plans).
  • Send important notifications about your account or subscription.

3. Sharing Data

We do not sell, trade, or transfer user data to third parties outside the scope of the extension's functions. All data remains private and secure.

4. Permissions

The extension may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save user preferences and subscription status.
  • Notifications: To alert users of domain availability or subscription updates.
  • Host permissions: To fetch domain registration and availability info from trusted sources.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us at Contact@servpix.com.
  • Opt-out of notifications at any time.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
